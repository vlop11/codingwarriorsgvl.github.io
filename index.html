WHY should I join?

Technology is becoming a super important part of our modern society, and learning to code is a great skill to have. Our club is open to all members of any skill level, so no matter how much you know, you're bound to learn more. Joining the Coding Warriors means joining a community focused on creating projects and getting to know new people with your same interests. If you're interested in coding, even if computer science isn't your major, join our club to learn and meet with a group of a people all focused on the same goal!
